How it works

Same trust as using your card, minus the card.

The vendor's phone does the talking to our servers. You just need your code and your PIN, that's it.

01

Share your code

Signup and get your unique wallet code. It works without network, and it doesn't change. It's just like having a digital ATM card.

02

Vendor enters the amount

The driver or vendor types in what you owe on their own connected device, e.g ₦200 for shuttle, or ₦1,000 for a haircut, whatever the price is.

03

You confirm with your PIN

Type your PIN on their screen to authorize it. Nobody can charge your wallet without it, and every charge is capped.
