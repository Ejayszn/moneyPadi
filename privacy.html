Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July, 2026

MoneyPadi is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our cardless wallet and digital payment ecosystem, we collect the following datasets:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, and authentication status managed securely via Firebase Authentication.
  • Financial & Payout Data: Bank account numbers, bank codes, and verified account names. We securely save generated Paystack transfer recipient strings inside private database documents to process external payouts.
  • Transaction Ledgers: Real-time histories of deposits (top-ups), transactions, vendor payments, and withdrawal statuses tracked within our system.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to power core application features safely:

  • To instantly adjust and track your digital wallet balance records.
  • To execute backend webhook verifications so operations process even if a browser is closed unexpectedly.
  • To secure transaction paths against double-crediting via severe server-side constraints.
  • To present transparent operational histories natively on your profile dashboard.

3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Processors

We do not sell your personal or financial histories. Data updates flow out only to verified architecture operators:

Firebase (Google)

Hosts our cloud databases, real-time balance structures, user authentication states, and secure serverless backend logic.

Paystack Payments Ltd

Processes incoming funding checkouts, validates name-lookup queries, and securely delivers external transfer payouts.

4. Cryptographic Safety & Idempotency

All system paths utilize strict single-execution (idempotent) database transaction locks tied to unique transaction handles. Any attempt to duplicate system inputs or alter ledger values externally is automatically blocked by backend validation rules.
