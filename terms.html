1. Service Architecture & Pooled Funds

MoneyPadi acts as an accumulate-and-withdraw internal ledger program. Wallet statistics and numbers rendered on your active interface layout represent accurate internal ledger assertions backed by a pooled business processing fund maintained securely within our settlement partner accounts.

Withdrawal Threshold: Payout requests to destination bank accounts carry an absolute minimum floor limit of ₦500 per single run.

2. Top-Up Fees & Platform Maintenance Margin

To keep MoneyPadi running smoothly across campus, a flat ₦10 platform maintenance fee is applied to every incoming top-up transaction. When funding your wallet via our third-party payment gateway, this ₦10 margin will be added to your selected deposit amount at checkout, ensuring your requested balance hits your wallet in full.

3. External Withdrawal Processing Fees

When you transfer funds out of your wallet ledger into a local bank account, processing fees are explicitly deducted by our third-party processors directly from your total payout value based on the operational tier below:

Withdrawal Amount Range Fixed Processing Fee
₦5,000 or below ₦10
₦5,001 – ₦50,000 ₦25
Above ₦50,000 ₦50

Nigeria Tax Act 2025 Stamp Duty: In alignment with statutory finance regulations, any single bank transfer payload that touches or scales past ₦10,000 attracts an additional government-mandated ₦50 stamp duty. These totals are completely passed through and absorbed directly by the user during checkout confirmation mapping.

4. Immediate Debits & Automatic Fallbacks

When you request a payout, our server coordinates execute an instant optimistic debit on your internal system balance numbers. Because routing into local bank infrastructure resolves asynchronously, if the target network fails or returns an error event, your wallet is automatically re-credited for the full amount (including calculated fees) via system fallback processing hooks.

5. Identity & Verification Checks

Users bear complete accountability for inputting correct banking details. MoneyPadi invokes real-time name-matching lookups prior to saving recipient vectors to prevent typo errors. MoneyPadi is not liable for transactions sent to incorrect numbers due to typographical errors entered by the user.
